Fred Charles Arthur Ball B.E.M.

8th May 1923 - 20th February 2003.

30 years in the Royal Navy.

This is diary of my late fathers life in the Royal Navy. He never spoke in detail to me about his career, it was only after death that I felt this web site should be produced as a record based on the many photos he had kept and the diary he produced in 1988.
